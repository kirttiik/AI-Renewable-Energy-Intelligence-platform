# 📖 Data Dictionary

**Khavda Renewable Energy Digital Twin**

This document provides a comprehensive overview of the primary datasets utilized and generated by the analytics platform.

---

## 1. Raw & Processed Inputs

### `khavda_weather.csv`
* **Purpose:** Contains raw and cleaned meteorological observations from the NASA POWER API.
* **Key Columns:**
  * `date` (Datetime): Observation date.
  * `temperature_2m` (Float): Surface temperature in Celsius.
  * `all_sky_surface_shortwave_downward_irradiance` (Float): Solar radiation proxy.
  * `wind_speed_10m` (Float): Wind speed at 10 meters.
  * `cloud_amount` (Float): Percentage of cloud cover.
* **Business Meaning:** Forms the foundational physical truth required to simulate and forecast generation.

### `khavda_generation.csv`
* **Purpose:** Synthesized historical ground-truth generation data.
* **Key Columns:**
  * `date` (Datetime): Operational date.
  * `solar_generation_mw` (Float): Actual solar power produced.
  * `wind_generation_mw` (Float): Actual wind power produced.
  * `total_generation_mw` (Float): Blended output.
* **Business Meaning:** The target variables used to train the machine learning forecasting models.

---

## 2. Analytics Outputs

### `weather_risk_analytics.csv`
* **Purpose:** Classifies daily weather anomalies and their associated operational risks.
* **Key Columns:**
  * `overall_risk_level` (String): LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH.
  * `risk_alert` (String): Categorical alert status (e.g., CRITICAL_WEATHER_ALERT).
  * `active_high_risk_factors` (String): Specific triggers (e.g., CLOUD_CURTAILMENT, HEATWAVE).
* **Business Meaning:** Drives preventative maintenance and calculates revenue exposure.

### `carbon_offset_analytics.csv`
* **Purpose:** Translates energy generation into environmental impact metrics.
* **Key Columns:**
  * `co2_avoided_tons` (Float): Metric tons of CO₂ emissions prevented.
  * `coal_saved_tons` (Float): Equivalent tons of coal not burned.
  * `trees_equivalent_million` (Float): Equivalent carbon sequestration by trees.
* **Business Meaning:** Automates ESG reporting and carbon credit baseline tracking.

### `revenue_analytics.csv`
* **Purpose:** Maps megawatt generation to financial benchmarks.
* **Key Columns:**
  * `daily_revenue_inr` (Float): Total baseline revenue generated.
  * `revenue_at_risk_inr` (Float): Revenue potentially lost due to active weather risks.
  * `daily_revenue_crores` (Float): Formatted revenue for executive dashboards.
* **Business Meaning:** Bridges the gap between engineering outputs and corporate finance.

---

## 3. Forecasting Outputs

### `solar_predictions.csv` / `wind_predictions.csv` / `total_output_predictions.csv`
* **Purpose:** The final output of the XGBoost machine learning models.
* **Key Columns:**
  * `date` (Datetime): Target forecast date.
  * `actual_generation_mw` (Float): The true holdout generation value.
  * `predicted_generation_mw` (Float): The AI-predicted generation value.
  * `error_mw` (Float): Absolute deviation.
* **Business Meaning:** Used directly by grid dispatchers to schedule day-ahead power commitments.

---

## 4. Master Consolidation

### `executive_summary.csv`
* **Purpose:** A fully flattened, unified dataset merging all outputs for business intelligence tools.
* **Key Columns:**
  * `date` (Datetime): Primary key.
  * `predicted_total_generation_mw` (Float): Forecasted output.
  * `daily_revenue_inr` (Float): Financial performance.
  * `co2_avoided_tons` (Float): ESG performance.
  * `ai_insight` (String): Plain-English generative explanation of daily operations.
* **Business Meaning:** The single source of truth for Power BI and Streamlit dashboards.
